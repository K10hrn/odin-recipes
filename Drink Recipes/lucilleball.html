Lucille Ball

Ingredients

For the strawberry syrup

  • 1/2 cup/ 100g sugar
  • 5 large strawberries, hulled and quartered

Bring sugar and 1/2 cup/ 120ml water to a boil. Take off heat. Add strawberries and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes. Strain to remove the strawberry chunks.

For the cocktail

  • 1 750ml bottle rosé
  • 2 shots strawberry syrup
  • 2 shots fresh lemon juice
  • 2 shots vodka
  • Garnish: fresh strawberry

Pour rosé into ice cube trays and freeze overnight. In a blender, add rosé cubes, strawberry syrup, fresh lemon juice, and vodka. Blend for 2 minutes, until smooth. Pour into a wine glass and garnish with a fresh strawberry.
