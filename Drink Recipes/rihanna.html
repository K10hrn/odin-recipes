The Rihanna

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 shots gold rum
  • 1 1/2 shots coconut water
  • 1/2 shot lime juice
  • 1/2 shot pineapple juice
  • 1/2 shots agave syrup
  • Garnish: edible orchid

Add all ingredients to an ice-filled shaker and shake. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with an edible orchid.
