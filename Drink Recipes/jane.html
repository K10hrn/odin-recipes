The Jane Goodall

Ingredients

  • 2 shots light rum
  • 1 shot Cointreau
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 shot fresh lime juice
  • 1 tsp agave syrup
  • Garnish: lime wedge

Add all ingredients to a blender with 1/2 cup/ 100g ice and blend until smooth. Pour into a hurricane glass and garnish with a lime wedge and a straw.
