The Daryl Hannah

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 shots vodka
  • 3 shots tomato juice
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • Pinch celery salt
  • Pinch ground pepper
  • Hot sauce to taste
  • Horseradish to taste
  • Garnish: 2 stalks picked asparagus and a lemon wedge

Add all ingredients to a Mason jar full of ice and stir until mixed. Garnish with 2 stalks of pickled asparagus and a lemon edge.
