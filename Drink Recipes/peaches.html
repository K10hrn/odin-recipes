Peaches

Ingredients

For the Peach Puree

  • 1 ripe peach
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Peel and core peach and combine with lemon juice and sugar in a blender. Blend until smooth and strain.

For the cocktail

  • 1 shot peace puree
  • Prosecco
  • Garnish: peach slice

Pour peach puree into a champagne flute and top up with prosecco. Garnish with a peach slice.
