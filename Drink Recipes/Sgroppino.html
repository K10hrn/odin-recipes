Sgroppino (an Italian Cocktail)

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces frozen lemon sorbet
  • 8 ounces Prosecco
  • 2 ounces vodka
  • Mint leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Scoop the sorbet into a large metal bowl. Add one-half of the Prosecco and whisk it around until you have a smooth icy mixture.
  2. Whisk in the vodka and remaining Prosecco. The cocktail should be very slushy.
  3. Pour into champagne flutes or other pretty glasses. Garnish with fresh mint and a few blueberries. Or, pour into jars and place them in the freezer until ready to serve.
