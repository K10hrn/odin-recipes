The Angelina Jolie

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, chopped into small pieces
  • 1 lime (quartered)
  • 6 to 8 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 shots light rum
  • Club soda (soda water)
  • Garnish: mint sprig

Place sugar and lemongrass into a Collins grass. Muddle thoroughly with a muddler (lemongrass needs a bit of work!). Squeeze lime quarters into a glass, add mint leaves, and muddle more. Add rum, stir, and add ice to glass. Top with club soda and garnish with a mint sprig.
