The Serena Williams

Ingredients

  • 1 strawberry
  • 2 mint sprig
  • 2 shots Pimm's No. 1
  • Ginger Beer
  • Garnish: strawberry, mint sprig

Cut 1 strawberry lengthwise into several slices and pull the leaves off the mint sprig. Add sliced strawberry and mint leaves to a Collins glass and lightly muddle. Add 2 shots of Pimm's No. 1, fill the glass with ice, and top with ginger beer. Garnish with a strawberry cut so it can be balanced on the glass's rim, and a second mint sprig pushed down into the glass.
