The Flo-Jo

Ingredients

For the raspberry syrup

  • 1/2 cup /100g sugar
  • 1/4 cup /30g raspberries

Bring 1/2 cup /120ml water and sugar to a boil. Take off heat. Add raspberries and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes. Strain to remove the raspberry chunks.

For the cocktail

  • 1 shot raspberry syrup
  • 1/2 shot blue Curacao
  • 3 shots lemonade
  • 1 1/2 shots vodka

Fill a highball glass with crushed ice. Pour in raspberry syrup. Slowly pour in the blue Curacao on top, to layer. In a separate glass, mix together lemonade and vodka, and slowly pour into glass over top of the blue Curacao. Serve with a straw and a spoon -the drink looks pretty layered, but you'll want to stir it up before you drink it.
